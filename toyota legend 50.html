TOYOTA LEGEND 50

DESCRIPTION

At the front-end, a glossy blacked out grille makes the Legend 50 look mean and imposing. Stylish silver accent lines add visual flair to the fog lamp area, while LED headlamps with daytime running lamps take care of modernising the Legend 50s tough-guy demeanour. The Legend 50's side profile is adorned with black protective mouldings, chrome mirrors and black side steps as well as a sports bar with Legend 50 badging. A textured, heavy-duty, lockable tonneau cover keeps whatever you're transporting safe and dry. The rear has a black bumper, Legend 50 badging, and a standard towbar. The Legend 50 has exclusive dual-toned 18-inch alloy wheels with 265-60-R18 tyres that offer plenty of grip.
